### ⚡ **ENERGY EFFICIENCY - Use Less, Do More!**

**At Home:**
- Turn off lights when leaving room 💡 (saves electrical energy)
- Use LED bulbs (use 75% less energy!)
- Close fridge quickly 🧊 (saves cooling energy)
- Take shorter showers 🚿 (saves heating energy)
- Ride bike instead of car 🚴 (saves fuel energy)

**At School:**
- Walk to school 🚶 (saves vehicle fuel)
- Use natural light when possible ☀️
- Turn off computer properly (don't sleep-mode!)
- Reuse water bottles (saves energy making new bottles)

**Effect:**
- One person saving energy seems small
- BUT millions of people doing it = HUGE impact!
- Helps environment AND saves money! 💰

---

**Experiment 2: Device Energy Consumption Detective (30 min)**

**Materials Needed:**
- Kill-a-Watt meter (electricity measuring device)
- Various household devices

**Steps:**
1. Plug device into Kill-a-Watt meter
2. Run device for 1 hour
3. Read watts used
4. Calculate cost (watts × hours ÷ 1000 × $0.12/kwh = cost)
5. Compare devices

**Example Results:**
- LED bulb 💡 = 10 watts/hour = $0.00012/hour
- Incandescent bulb = 60 watts/hour = $0.0007/hour
- LED wins! Use 6x less energy!
